1.Enter how many you have
2.Search the internet and enter the consumption in watt of that device
To do that, search for the device brand and model on the internet
Then find where it specifies the consumption in watt of the device and enter it
If for instance, the program asks you to enter what is the consumption of a 
refrigirator, and after you search on the internet and find that it consumes
between 100-200wats in an hour, enter the value like this: 100-200w.
